Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are not allowed in the dumpsters?

Hazardous items such as chemicals, batteries, and tires are prohibited. Our team provides a full list upon booking.

How soon can I get a dumpster delivered?

Same-day delivery is available for most orders, depending on scheduling and availability.

How long can I keep the dumpster?

Rental periods are flexible, ranging from short-term use to extended projects. We’ll work with you to match your schedule.

What information should I have ready when I call to order a dumpster?

To help us provide the fastest and most accurate quote, it's helpful to have a general idea of the type of project you're working on (e.g., renovation, cleanout), the main types of debris you'll be disposing of, and the address for the delivery location. This information helps us recommend the perfect rolloff size for your needs.

Can I mix yard waste with my household junk in the same dumpster?

In most cases, yes. You can typically mix general yard waste like branches and leaves with household items and construction debris. However, if you have a very large volume of heavy organic material like dirt or stumps, please let us know when you order, as special handling may be required.

What happens if your delivery truck arrives and can't place the dumpster?

If we are unable to complete a delivery or pickup due to an obstructed area (e.g., a car is in the way, or a gate is locked), a trip fee may be assessed to cover the fuel and labor for the attempted service. It is very important to ensure the area is clear and accessible on your scheduled service day.
